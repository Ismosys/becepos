Service

Reputation Recovery

Bad weeks happen. A staff change, a viral complaint, a season that did not go to plan. Recovery is a focused project that brings the rating, the review velocity and the search results back to where they belong.

Overview

Recovery is different from steady-state reputation work. The clock is short. The audience is watching. The team is stressed. Our job is to stabilize the public picture while you fix what needs fixing internally.

Every recovery engagement starts with the same question. What is the root cause, and is it resolved? Reputation work cannot paint over an operational problem. Once the cause is addressed, we move fast on the public side.

What you get

  • Same-day reputation audit and stabilization plan
  • Calm, accountable replies to every active negative review
  • Platform flagging on any reviews that violate site policy, with evidence
  • Accelerated review request flow for verified recent customers
  • Search results review, including suppression of obsolete negative coverage
  • Owner-facing playbook so the team is aligned on what to say where

Our process

  1. Stabilize

    Within 48 hours we have read every active negative review, met with leadership and drafted the first wave of responses.

  2. Address

    Public reply, internal follow-up and policy flagging where appropriate. We document everything.

  3. Accelerate

    Recent satisfied customers are invited to share their experience. Volume returns and the rating starts moving back up.

  4. Compound

    The recovery project transitions into ongoing Review Management once the rating has stabilized.

What recovery is not

We do not delete real reviews. We do not bury negative coverage with fake content. We follow platform policy. The work that produces lasting results is operational improvement plus disciplined public communication, in that order.
