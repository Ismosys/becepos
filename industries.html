Built for Businesses Where Reputation Is the Product

Reviews matter to every business. They matter more in some industries than others. These are the ones we work with most.

Restaurants and food service

Every full table is a chance for a review. Every empty Friday is a question mark. We build a flow that asks guests at the right moment, replies to every star and protects the front-of-house team from the comments that turn into complaints.

  • POS-triggered review requests timed to the end of service
  • Google, OpenTable, Yelp, TripAdvisor and Facebook coverage
  • Food-allergy and dietary mention monitoring with operational alerts
  • Photo coordination with kitchen for Google Business Profile

Hotels and hospitality

Hotel reviews are operational data. Slow check-in, noisy AC, breakfast hours, parking. We tag every review with the operational issue it surfaces, reply in-brand and feed weekly themes to the GM.

  • Booking.com, Expedia, TripAdvisor, Google, Hotels.com coverage
  • Post-stay request flow timed to check-out
  • Operational sentiment dashboard for the GM
  • Photo and Google Posts cadence for seasonal campaigns

Law firms

Legal reviews require care. Ethics rules vary by jurisdiction. We work with your compliance lead to build a request flow that respects bar rules, captures consenting clients and handles confidentiality with discipline.

  • Google, Avvo, Martindale, Lawyers.com coverage
  • Bar-rule-aware request templates and response guidelines
  • Confidentiality-first response protocols on every reply
  • Local pack ranking for practice area plus city keywords

Dental clinics

Patients trust other patients. We integrate review requests into the appointment cycle, never leaving the front desk to guess when to ask. Reviews are pulled to the new-patient landing pages, which compounds the SEO impact.

  • Google, Healthgrades, ZocDoc, RateMDs coverage
  • HIPAA-aware reply guidelines
  • Review-rich landing pages by service and by location
