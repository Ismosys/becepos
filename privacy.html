Privacy Policy

This page explains what information Becepos collects, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information we collect

When you contact us through this website, we collect the information you provide on the form: your name, business name, email address, optional phone number and the content of your message. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics tags on this site.

During a client engagement we receive access to your review platform accounts, customer feedback data, and any documents you share with us for the purpose of running the program.

2. How we use information

We use the information you share to respond to your inquiry, prepare your audit, and (if you become a client) deliver the services we have agreed to deliver. We never sell, rent or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. How we store and protect information

Client information is stored in access-controlled systems used by our team. Customer feedback data is treated as confidential and is never shared with parties outside the engagement without your written approval. We retain client records only as long as is required to deliver services and to comply with applicable accounting and legal obligations.

4. Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of the information we hold about you at any time. Send a request to ismaellateef81@gmail.com and we will respond within five working days.

5. Third-party platforms

To deliver reputation management services we work inside platforms operated by third parties (for example Google Business Profile, Facebook, Trustpilot, Yelp and TripAdvisor). Those platforms have their own privacy policies which govern how they handle the information you and your customers submit to them. We recommend reading those policies as well.

6. Children

This website is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 16.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy should be sent to ismaellateef81@gmail.com.
